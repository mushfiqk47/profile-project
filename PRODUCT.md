# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Busy urban dwellers looking for quick, high-quality, organic local grocery delivery with premium visual warmth and speed.

## Product Purpose
FreshCart provides neighborhood market delivery, bringing fresh, local organic produce and grocery essentials to the user's door in 30 minutes. Success is represented by a seamless shopping experience, clear cart management, effortless checkout, and transparent real-time tracking.

## Brand Personality
Premium, warm, artisanal. Evokes local craftsmanship, clean editorial restraint, and organic warmth with an efficient, premium modern delivery vibe.

## Anti-references
- SaaS UI clichés: Generic white layouts, thin/gray text with low contrast, overused glassmorphism blurs, capitalized tracked eyebrows on every section, colored left-border side-stripes.
- Cheap discount-supermarket templates: Cluttered high cognitive load grids, neon warning red/yellow starburst badges, and generic templates.

## Design Principles
1. **Artisanal Restraint**: Embrace a natural, premium aesthetic with warm, high-contrast typography and clear layout grids. Avoid over-scaffolding and decorative slop.
2. **Immediate Visibility**: Never gate content visibility behind transitions. Keep essential information readable immediately.
3. **Workflow Utility**: The design serves the user. Ensure interactive components (like stepper inputs and addresses) have high hit targets and zero dead zones.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Strict compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA:
- Contrast ratios >= 4.5:1 for body and placeholder text.
- Comprehensive keyboard navigability and visible focus rings.
- Proper heading hierarchies (`<h1>` - `<h6>`) and semantic HTML tags.
- Explicit image width/height dimensions to prevent layout shifts (CLS).
- Decorative elements hidden from screen readers (`aria-hidden="true"`).

## Bangladeshi Localization
- **Currency**: Bangladeshi Taka (`৳`/BDT) prefix formatted without decimals. Comma grouping follows the South Asian standard (`en-IN` lakhs/crores formatting).
- **Regions**: Primary customer base located in urban Dhaka (Gulshan, Banani, Dhanmondi, Uttara, Lalmatia).
- **Payments**: Cash on Delivery (COD) is the default billing workflow, accompanied by Mobile Financial Services (MFS) integration (bKash, Nagad, Rocket).
- **Products**: Prioritize local deshi produce (Rajshahi mangoes, Padma Hilsha, Savar vegetables, Aarong dairy).
